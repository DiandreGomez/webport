LiftN'Learn: Interactive RFID-Powered Resource Display for Enhanced Learning Experiences in Early Education

INTRODUCTION

The goal of this project, LiftN’'Learn, is to provide a cutting-edge, engaging teaching resource especially for kids who struggle with mental health issues. The project aims to improve learning in educational environments by utilizing Arduino microcontroller systems and RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) technology.

OBJECTIVES

  1. Within three months of implementation, make sure that at least 40% more students with various learning needs exhibit increased course comprehension through their interactions with LiftNLearn.
  2. Achieve a 25% improvement in student engagement measures, as indicated by better participation rates, more time spent on task, and favorable instructor evaluation, within two months of implementing the interactive technologies.
  3. Implement positive reinforcement strategies to reduce disruptive behaviors, such as calling out, fidgeting, and frequent movement, by 20% within two months of deploying LiftN'Learn tools.
Aquino-Samontanes Dental Clinic: Appointments and Patient Records Management System

INTRODUCTION

The Aquino-Samontanes Dental Clinic offers comprehensive dental services and plans to enhance operations through a patient records management system and a web-based appointment scheduling program. These systems aim to streamline processes, ensure accurate patient data, maintain supply levels, and improve efficiency while providing personalized care and expanding community reach.

OBJECTIVES

  1. To implement a patient record management system within four months that maintains complete patient information after treatment while being 75% faster that the current manual process
  2. To develop an appointment system that allows for efficient and quick scheduling of patient visits within seconds.
  3. To develop a user-friendly system that provides clear instructions that minimizes the need for interviews by the dentist, resulting in minimizing of interviews conducted for gathering medical history information
  4. To implement a secure and organized method for attaching and storing X-ray films in the system to prevent loss or misplacement.
  5. To implement an organized patient record retrieval that reduces the time required to search for specific patient records in less than a minute.
Efficient Plant Watering Using an Arduino-Based Automatic Irrigation System

INTRODUCTION

This study aims to develop an efficient plant watering system using moisture sensors. The project automates the process of plant watering by continuously monitoring soil moisture levels and dispensing water accordingly.

OBJECTIVES

  1. Develop and construct an Arduino-based automatic irrigation system with accurate soil moisture measurement and real-time data monitoring.
  2. Create a user-friendly interface for customizable watering schedules, optimizing water usage efficiency, and incorporating sustainable features.
  3. Conduct thorough testing, documentation, and evaluation to ensure reliability, replication, and effectiveness of the irrigation system.
